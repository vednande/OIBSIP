Lal Bahadur Shastri
1904 - 1966
We believe in peace and peaceful development, not only for ourselves but for people all over the world.

We must fight for peace bravely as we fought in the war.

Biography

Lal Bahadur Shastri was the second Prime Minister of independent India. He studied in East Central Railway Inter college and Harish Chandra High School, which he left to join the non-cooperation movement. Lal Bahadur Shastri was a very simple man who worked for the betterment of the country. He led the country successfully through Indo-Pakistan War in 1965. He popularized the slogan ‘Jai Jawan Jai Kisan’, recognizing the need for self-sustenance and self-reliance as the pillars to build a strong nation. Shastri tackled many elementary problems like food shortage, unemployment and poverty. To overcome the acute food shortage, Shastri asked the experts to devise a long-term strategy.
